Why UniVote?

Built to solve real problems in university elections

🎓

Student Identity Verification

Every voter's matric number is cryptographically hashed and bound to their wallet on-chain. One student, one wallet, one vote — enforced by the blockchain itself.

🔒

Tamper-Proof Voting

Votes are recorded permanently on the Ethereum blockchain. No admin, no authority, and no hacker can alter or delete a vote once it is cast.

🏅

NFT Voter Certificate

Every voter automatically receives a non-transferable NFT certificate after voting — a permanent, verifiable proof of participation on the blockchain.

📊

Real-Time Results

Election results are publicly visible and update in real time as votes are cast. No waiting for manual counting — the smart contract tallies everything automatically.

🌐

Fully Decentralized

No central server, no database, no single point of failure. The entire system runs on the Ethereum blockchain — available to anyone, anywhere, at any time.

✅

Independently Verifiable

Any student can verify their vote was counted using their transaction hash. No need to trust the university — the blockchain is the proof.

How It Works

1

Admin creates an election

The election officer creates an election with a title, description, start time and end time. Candidates are added before the election begins.

2

Students are registered with their matric number

Each eligible student's matric number is hashed and permanently bound to their wallet address on-chain. The same matric number cannot be used twice.

3

Students connect their wallet and vote

During the election period, registered students connect their MetaMask wallet and cast their vote. The smart contract verifies their eligibility instantly.

4

NFT certificate is minted automatically

The moment a vote is cast, a non-transferable NFT voter certificate is minted directly to the student's wallet as permanent proof of participation.

5

Results are publicly verifiable

After the election ends, the winner is determined automatically by the smart contract. Anyone can verify the results independently on the blockchain.
